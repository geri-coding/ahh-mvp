Our Story

Built From Research, Not Assumptions

Aura Healing Hub was born from over 18 months of deep community research — social listening, academic literature review, and direct conversations with people who have struggled to find mental health care that truly understands them.

We found the same story repeated across communities: brilliant, caring therapists on one side, and clients who had given up on finding someone who really gets them on the other. The gap wasn't willingness — it was visibility and trust.

That's what this platform solves. We're not a generic directory. We're a trust-building infrastructure for culturally competent care.

FAQ

Questions Therapists Ask Us

What happens to my license number after I submit?
▾
Your license number is used only for verification against your state's licensing board records. It is stored securely and never displayed on your public profile. Only your name, credentials (e.g. LCSW), and the information you choose to show are visible to clients.
Can I update or remove my listing after it goes live?
▾
Yes, always. You have full control over your profile at all times. You can update your specialties, photo, bio, availability, and session fees whenever you need to. You can also remove your listing instantly — no waiting period, no questions asked.
How is this different from Psychology Today?
▾
Psychology Today is a general-purpose directory. Aura Healing Hub is specifically built for clients who need culturally competent care — people who have historically struggled to find therapists who understand their background without explanation. Our filters, badges, and matching system are designed around cultural identity, not just insurance and zip code.
What is the Racial Trauma Specialist badge?
▾
The Racial Trauma Specialist badge is our highest-tier cultural competency credential, available to Premium listing therapists who have documented training or demonstrated expertise in racial trauma therapy. It signals to clients that you have specialized knowledge in this area — making your listing significantly more visible to clients specifically seeking this expertise.
Who reviews my application?
▾
Applications are currently reviewed personally by our founding team. We're a small, mission-driven operation — not an automated system. You'll receive a direct email response within 24 hours. If we have questions about your application, we'll reach out before making any decisions.
I have more questions. How do I reach you?
▾
Email us directly at hello@aurahealinghub.com. You can also connect with the founder on LinkedIn. We respond to every message personally — usually within one business day.
